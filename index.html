🧾 How the simple weekly system works
With only two weekly inputs, the app detects whole-system drift, not exact category overspend. If Offset + Bills/Savings is lower than expected, it flags a leak and tightens next week. It does not falsely claim “Living overspent” unless you enter transaction detail.
🏠 PPOR kill mission
